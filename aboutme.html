My name is Enkel Prifti. I am 17 years old and a high school senior at Columbia Secondary School. I have lived in several places but I am currently living in Pelham Parkway. During my time in this neighborhood, I have noticed that movie theaters, skateparks, and bike shops are missing. Before I moved here, I would skate and bike a lot but then I stopped and the main factor is that these places are missing in my neighborhood. Therefore, I have decided to make this website in order for people to show interest in these areas of entertainment to other companies so that they can build something in Pelham Parkway.
